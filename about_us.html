Tour de America

Michael Woodward & Valerie Torrez

Our story, our why, and what keeps us pedaling.

How we met

We crossed paths in Flagstaff in May 2025, instantly connecting over mountain air, long rides, and the idea of an adventure bigger than ourselves.

Why we ride

This Tour de America is about honoring family, celebrating resilience, and sharing the joy of the road. Each mile nods to the people we miss and the community we’re grateful for.

What to expect

  • Daily check-ins from the road: mileage, highlights, and lessons learned.
  • Ways to join us for a segment or send a note of encouragement.
  • Direct links to the causes we’re riding for.
